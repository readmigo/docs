## Quality Indicators Legend

- **Very High**: Peer-reviewed, widely adopted, professionally produced, high download counts
- **High**: Well-structured, accurate content, positive reviews, established publisher/institution
- **Medium-High**: Good quality but may be narrower in scope or less comprehensive
- **Medium**: Useful but may need supplementation, less polished
- **Variable**: Collection with mixed quality levels; verify individual items

## Notes for Readmigo Integration

### Format Availability
Most books are available in multiple formats suitable for e-readers:
- **PDF** - Universal format, fixed layout
- **EPUB** - Reflowable text, ideal for e-readers
- **HTML** - Web-based reading, often interactive
- **MOBI** - Kindle-compatible format

### Quality Indicators Used
- **University Adoption**: Used as course textbook at recognized institutions
- **GitHub Stars**: Community popularity metric (for open source projects)
- **Publisher Reputation**: Published by established publishers (O'Reilly, No Starch, MIT Press)
- **Author Credentials**: Written by recognized experts or academics
- **Community Maintenance**: Regularly updated and community-supported

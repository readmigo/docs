### Translation Quality Tiers

**Excellent (Multiple Quality Options)**:
- Greek classics (Homer, Plato, dramatists)
- Shakespeare (original English)
- Latin classics
- French classics

**Good (Serviceable PD Translations)**:
- Russian literature (Garnett translations)
- German philosophy (older academic translations)
- Asian classics (Legge, Muller editions)

**Variable (Check Specific Edition)**:
- Middle Eastern texts
- Religious texts (varies by tradition)
- Lesser-known European languages

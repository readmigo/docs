#### Psychology 2e (OpenStax)
- **Author:** Rose M. Spielman, William J. Jenkins, Marilyn D. Lovett et al.
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0)
- **URL:** https://openstax.org/details/books/psychology-2e
- **Description:** A comprehensive introduction to psychology designed to meet scope and sequence requirements for single-semester courses. Covers core concepts grounded in classic studies and current research, including DSM-5 coverage for psychological disorders. Incorporates diversity within the discipline and across cultures.
- **Quality Indicator:** Peer-reviewed, widely adopted in universities, part of OpenStax collection (Rice University)

#### Noba Psychology Modules
- **Author:** Various expert contributors (103+ modules)
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)
- **URL:** https://nobaproject.com/
- **Description:** A free online platform providing high-quality, flexibly structured psychology textbooks. Contains 90+ learning modules covering every area commonly taught in introductory psychology. Instructors can customize by rearranging or removing modules.
- **Quality Indicator:** Written by top experts in the field, highly customizable, peer-reviewed

#### Lifespan Development: A Psychological Perspective (4th Edition)
- **Author:** Martha Lally and Suzanne Valentine-French
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 3.0 (CC BY-NC-SA 3.0)
- **URL:** https://open.umn.edu/opentextbooks/textbooks/lifespan-development-a-psychological-perspective
- **Description:** Comprehensive coverage of human development from conception through death, examining physical, cognitive, and psychosocial development at each life stage.
- **Quality Indicator:** Multiple editions indicate ongoing updates, peer-reviewed

#### Research Methods in Psychology (4th Edition)
- **Author:** Rajiv S. Jhangiani, Carrie Cuttler, and Dana C. Leighton
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)
- **URL:** https://kpu.pressbooks.pub/psychmethods4e/
- **Description:** Comprehensive introduction to research methods in psychology for undergraduate students. Covers scientific method, ethics, measurement, experimental and non-experimental research designs.
- **Quality Indicator:** Published by Kwantlen Polytechnic University, peer-reviewed, multiple editions

#### Fundamentals of Psychological Disorders (3rd Edition)
- **Author:** Alexis Bridley and Lee Daffin
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)
- **URL:** https://opentext.wsu.edu/abnormal-psych/
- **Description:** Tackles mental disorders in 15 modules, updated through DSM-5-TR. Covers major psychological disorders with clinical examples and contemporary research.
- **Quality Indicator:** Updated to current diagnostic criteria, peer-reviewed

#### The Principles of Psychology (Volumes 1 & 2)
- **Author:** William James
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/57628
- **Description:** Foundational work in psychology written in the late 19th century. Addresses fundamental principles and scope of psychology as a discipline, linking mental processes to underlying physical conditions. One of the most influential psychology texts ever written.
- **Quality Indicator:** Foundational text, 8,000+ downloads on Project Gutenberg

#### The Varieties of Religious Experience
- **Author:** William James
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/621
- **Description:** A study in human nature examining religious experiences from a psychological perspective. Based on the Gifford Lectures delivered at Edinburgh University.
- **Quality Indicator:** Classic work, 2,977+ downloads

#### Psychology of the Unconscious
- **Author:** Carl Gustav Jung
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/65903
- **Description:** Jung's exploration of the unconscious mind, dreams, and symbolism. One of his most influential early works that helped establish analytical psychology.
- **Quality Indicator:** 3,384+ downloads, foundational text in analytical psychology

#### Collected Papers on Analytical Psychology
- **Author:** Carl Gustav Jung
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/48225
- **Description:** Collection of Jung's key papers on analytical psychology, including discussions of psychological types, the unconscious, and dream analysis.
- **Quality Indicator:** 1,923+ downloads

#### The Theory of Psychoanalysis
- **Author:** Carl Gustav Jung
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/66041
- **Description:** Jung's reflections on the foundations and transformations of psychoanalytic theory, including critiques and insights on Freud's work.
- **Quality Indicator:** Important historical document in psychology

#### A General Introduction to Psychoanalysis
- **Author:** Sigmund Freud
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/38219
- **Description:** Freud's comprehensive introduction to psychoanalytic theory, covering dreams, parapraxes, and the structure of the psyche.
- **Quality Indicator:** 2,618+ downloads, foundational text

#### Dream Psychology: Psychoanalysis for Beginners
- **Author:** Sigmund Freud
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/15489
- **Description:** Accessible introduction to Freud's theories of dream interpretation and the unconscious mind.
- **Quality Indicator:** 1,339+ downloads

#### Totem and Taboo
- **Author:** Sigmund Freud
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/41214
- **Description:** Freud's exploration of the resemblances between the psychic lives of "savages" and neurotics, examining totemism and taboo from a psychoanalytic perspective.
- **Quality Indicator:** 2,895+ downloads

#### Human Nature and Conduct
- **Author:** John Dewey
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/41386
- **Description:** An introduction to social psychology exploring human habits, impulses, and intelligence in social contexts.
- **Quality Indicator:** 2,502+ downloads, important work in pragmatist psychology

#### Introduction to Sociology 3e (OpenStax)
- **Author:** Tonja R. Conerly, Kathleen Holmes, and Asha Lal Tamang
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0)
- **URL:** https://openstax.org/details/books/introduction-sociology-3e
- **Description:** Comprehensive introduction to sociology covering foundational theories (functionalism, conflict theory, symbolic interactionism) and newer paradigms (feminist theory, critical race theory). Addresses contemporary social issues and cultural diversity.
- **Quality Indicator:** OpenStax peer-reviewed, widely adopted, also available as audiobook

#### Classical Sociological Theory and Foundations of American Sociology
- **Author:** Allison Hurst
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-SA 4.0)
- **URL:** https://open.oregonstate.education/sociologicaltheory/
- **Description:** Divided into four sections corresponding to Marx, Weber, and Durkheim, plus early American sociologists. Provides detailed analysis of theorists' lives and how their experiences influenced their work. Includes primary source readings.
- **Quality Indicator:** Oregon State University publication, comprehensive primary sources

#### The Sociological Theory Reader
- **Author:** Alecea Ritter Standlee
- **License:** Creative Commons (Open Access)
- **URL:** https://cupola.gettysburg.edu/oer/12/
- **Description:** Brings together open-access excerpts from key figures in sociological theory including Marx, Gilman, Addams, Durkheim, Cooley, Weber, and DuBois.
- **Quality Indicator:** Gettysburg College publication, curated primary sources

#### Principles of Sociological Inquiry
- **Author:** Saylor Academy
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 (CC BY 3.0)
- **URL:** https://www.saylor.org/books/
- **Description:** Introduction to sociological research methods and inquiry, covering both qualitative and quantitative approaches.
- **Quality Indicator:** Saylor Academy certified course material

#### Social Problems: Continuity and Change
- **Author:** University of Minnesota Libraries
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)
- **URL:** https://open.umn.edu/opentextbooks/textbooks/social-problems-continuity-and-change
- **Description:** Examines major social problems in contemporary society including poverty, crime, health, education, and environmental issues.
- **Quality Indicator:** Peer-reviewed, Open Textbook Library

#### The Elementary Forms of the Religious Life
- **Author:** Emile Durkheim
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/41360
- **Description:** Durkheim's exploration of the most primitive forms of religion, analyzing the essential components of religious experience and its fundamental role in society. Examines totemism as a window into understanding social solidarity.
- **Quality Indicator:** 8,878+ downloads, foundational text in sociology of religion

#### Le Suicide: Etude de Sociologie
- **Author:** Emile Durkheim
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/author/40654
- **Description:** Durkheim's groundbreaking study applying sociological methods to analyze suicide rates, demonstrating how social factors influence individual behavior. (Available in French)
- **Quality Indicator:** Foundational text in sociological methodology

#### The Communist Manifesto
- **Author:** Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/61
- **Description:** The foundational document of communist theory, analyzing the class struggle and proposing revolutionary change.
- **Quality Indicator:** One of the most influential political documents in history

#### Capital, Volume 1
- **Author:** Karl Marx
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1867-c1/
- **Description:** Marx's comprehensive analysis of capitalist production, commodity exchange, and labor theory of value.

#### Introduction to Anthropology (OpenStax)
- **Author:** Jennifer Hasty, David G. Lewis, and Marjorie M. Snipes
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0)
- **URL:** https://openstax.org/details/books/introduction-anthropology
- **Description:** A four-field anthropology text integrating diverse voices, engaging field activities, and themes like Indigenous experiences and social inequality. Over 600 pages covering biological, archaeological, linguistic, and cultural anthropology.
- **Quality Indicator:** OpenStax peer-reviewed, comprehensive four-field approach

#### Perspectives: An Open Introduction to Cultural Anthropology (2nd Edition)
- **Author:** Nina Brown, Thomas McIlwraith, Laura Tubelle de Gonzalez (Editors)
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 (CC BY-NC 4.0)
- **URL:** https://perspectives.americananthro.org/
- **Description:** The first peer-reviewed open-access textbook for cultural anthropology. Edited volume with each chapter written by different expert authors. Produced by the Society for Anthropology in Community Colleges.
- **Quality Indicator:** American Anthropological Association publication, peer-reviewed, includes diverse authors

#### The Art of Being Human: A Textbook for Cultural Anthropology
- **Author:** Michael Wesch
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)
- **URL:** https://newprairiepress.org/ebooks/
- **Description:** Unique anthropology textbook where the author shares personal adventures as an anthropologist, exploring what the science of human beings can tell us about the art of being human.
- **Quality Indicator:** New Prairie Press publication, engaging narrative approach

#### Explorations: An Open Invitation to Biological Anthropology
- **Author:** Beth Shook, Katie Nelson, Kelsie Aguilera, and Lara Braff (Editors)
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 (CC BY-NC 4.0)

- **Description:** The first comprehensive, peer-reviewed open-access textbook for biological anthropology. Covers human evolution, primatology, genetics, and human variation.
- **Quality Indicator:** Society for Anthropology in Community Colleges publication, peer-reviewed

#### Native Peoples of North America

#### The Prince
- **Author:** Niccolo Machiavelli
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/1232
- **Description:** The foundational text of modern political philosophy, written in 1513. Offers pragmatic advice on how rulers can acquire and maintain political power. Revolutionary in its separation of politics from ethics.
- **Quality Indicator:** One of the most influential political treatises ever written

#### Discourses on the First Decade of Titus Livius
- **Author:** Niccolo Machiavelli
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/10827
- **Description:** Machiavelli's analysis of republican government based on Roman history. Often considered companion to The Prince, showing his support for republican ideals.
- **Quality Indicator:** 1,955+ downloads, essential for understanding Machiavelli's full political thought

#### Second Treatise of Government
- **Author:** John Locke
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/7370
- **Description:** Foundational text of liberal political philosophy published in 1690. Critiques divine right of kings, presents government by consent, natural rights, and the right of revolution. Influenced the American Declaration of Independence.
- **Quality Indicator:** Foundational text of modern liberalism and constitutional government

#### The Social Contract & Discourses
- **Author:** Jean-Jacques Rousseau
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/46333
- **Description:** Rousseau's exploration of legitimate political authority, the general will, and popular sovereignty. Synthesizes Hobbes's absolute sovereignty with Locke's popular consent into the doctrine of popular sovereignty.
- **Quality Indicator:** Essential Enlightenment political philosophy

#### Emile; Or, Concerning Education
- **Author:** Jean-Jacques Rousseau
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/author/1286
- **Description:** Rousseau's treatise on education and human nature. He considered it the "best and most important" of all his writings. Explores natural education and child development.
- **Quality Indicator:** 1,086+ downloads, foundational text in educational philosophy

#### A Discourse Upon the Origin and Foundation of Inequality Among Mankind
- **Author:** Jean-Jacques Rousseau
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/11136
- **Description:** Rousseau's examination of social inequality, arguing that civilization has corrupted natural human goodness.
- **Quality Indicator:** 1,757+ downloads

#### On Liberty
- **Author:** John Stuart Mill
- **License:** Public Domain
- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/34901

- **Quality Indicator:** Essential text on liberal political philosophy

#### Utilitarianism

- **URL:** https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/11224

#### Considerations on Representative Government

## Quality Indicators Used

- **Peer-reviewed**: Reviewed by academic experts before publication
- **Download counts**: High download numbers from Project Gutenberg indicate popularity and accessibility
- **Institutional backing**: Published by universities or academic organizations (OpenStax/Rice, Oregon State, etc.)
- **Multiple editions**: Indicates ongoing maintenance and updates
- **Wide adoption**: Used in college courses across multiple institutions
- **Open license**: Legal clarity for reuse and adaptation
